Local Nature Recovery calculator Check your funding options

Local Nature Recovery is a new scheme that replaces Countryside Stewardship grants.

The scheme will pay for actions that support local nature recovery and deliver local environmental priorities.

Actions covered by the scheme include:

  • creating, managing and restoring natural habitats
  • connecting isolated habitats to form networks
  • natural flood management
  • species management
  • rights of way navigation and recreation infrastructure
  • education infrastructure, events and services
  • geodiversity and heritage asset management

Use our Local Nature Recovery calculator to find out:

  • whether you're eligible
  • how much you could earn
  • where to apply
